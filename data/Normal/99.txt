Lisa a 50-year-old woman living in a metropolitan area known for its high levels of air pollution. She has a family history of heart disease, with both her father and brother having suffered heart attacks at a young age. Recently, Lisa underwent genetic testing, which revealed a high polygenic risk score for coronary artery disease (CAD), despite her not having any symptoms or a diagnosis of CAD. Concerned about her genetic predisposition and the impact of her environment, she decided to focus on preventative measures. Lisa adopted a heart-healthy lifestyle, incorporating regular exercise, a balanced diet rich in fruits and vegetables, and mindfulness practices to manage stress. Her healthcare provider also recommended routine check-ups to monitor her cardiovascular health, emphasizing the importance of reducing exposure to air pollution and maintaining a healthy lifestyle.
