Narjis, A 60-year-old female presented with chest discomfort after engaging in heavy lifting while gardening. She described the pain as a tightness in her chest, which led her to fear she might be experiencing a heart attack. An ECG was performed, which showed normal findings. Cardiac enzyme tests were also negative. A musculoskeletal examination indicated strain in the pectoral muscles due to recent physical exertion.  The patient was advised on proper lifting techniques and given a regimen of rest and ice application for muscle strain relief. She reported improvement in her symptoms within a few days.
