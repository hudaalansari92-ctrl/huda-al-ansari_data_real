Hassan, a 45-year-old man, has a strong family history of coronary artery disease, as his father and grandfather died from heart attacks at a young age. Despite this family history, Mr. Hassan does not suffer from any symptoms indicating coronary artery disease, such as chest pain or shortness of breath, and he has not been diagnosed with the disease. His lifestyle is somewhat balanced, as he exercises irregularly and follows a moderate diet, but he feels anxious due to his family history. Based on his medical consultation, he was advised to regularly monitor cholesterol and blood pressure levels and maintain a healthy lifestyle that includes regular exercise and consuming heart-healthy foods. Although he has not yet contracted coronary artery disease, Hassan follows the doctor's preventive recommendations to reduce the likelihood of developing the disease in the future.
